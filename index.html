01 — About

Bridging psychology, neuroscience, and code.

I'm a neurobehavioral analyst and neuroscience researcher with over five years of experience spanning EEG, fMRI, and computational methods. At the Child Mind Institute, I supervise fMRI datasets, build automation scripts, and run quality control — alongside biweekly assessments and support for sleep studies.

My work draws on quantitative EEG analysis, cognitive assessment, and neurodiversity-focused research.

I focus on sensory processing in autism and on teaching methods that support diverse learners. By integrating psychology and neuroscience, I work to turn rigorous findings into tools that create tangible benefits in clinical and educational settings.

That same commitment carries into leadership and advocacy — through fellowships, community initiatives, and the nonprofit I founded and direct.
